Deep learning articles

Reading list:

  • LeCun, Y., Bottou, L., Bengio, Y., & Haffner, P. (1998).
    Gradient-based learning applied to document recognition Proceedings of the IEEE, Vol. 86, pp. 2278–2324.

  • Bengio, Y. et al. (2003).
    A Neural Probabilistic Language Model. J. Mach. Learn. Res. 3.

  • Krizhevsky, A., Sutskever, I., & Hinton, G. E. (2012).
    ImageNet Classification with Deep Convolutional Neural Networks (AlexNet). In Proceedings of the 25th International Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems (NIPS), Vol. 1, pp. 1097–1105.

  • Szegedy, C., Liu, W., Jia, Y., Sermanet, P., Reed, S., Anguelov, D., Erhan, D., Vanhoucke, V., & Rabinovich, A. (2015).
    Going deeper with convolutions (Inception/GoogLeNet). 2015 IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), pp. 1–9.

  • Schroff, F., Kalenichenko, D., & Philbin, J. (2015).
    FaceNet: A Unified Embedding for Face Recognition and Clustering (FaceNet). In Proceedings of the Proceedings of the IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR).

  • Ronneberger, O., Fischer, P., & Brox, T. (2015).
    U-Net: Convolutional Networks for Biomedical Image Segmentation (U-Net). In Proceedings of the Medical Image Computing and Computer-Assisted Intervention -- MICCAI 2015, pp. 234–241.

  • He, K., Zhang, X., Ren, S., & Sun, J. (2016).
    Deep Residual Learning for Image Recognition (ResNet). 2016 IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), pp. 770–778.

  • Kingma, D. P., & Ba, J. (2017).
    Adam: A Method for Stochastic Optimization (Adam). In 5th International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR).

  • Vaswani, A., Shazeer, N., Parmar, N., Uszkoreit, J., Jones, L., Gomez, A. N., Kaiser, Ł., & Polosukhin, I. (2017).
    Attention Is All You Need (Transformers). In Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS), Vol. 30.

  • Kingma, D. P., & Welling, M. (2019).
    Auto-Encoding Variational Bayes (Variational Autoencoder). In International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR).

  • Ho, J., Jain, A., & Abbeel, P. (2020).
    Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Models (Variational Autoencoder). In Proceedings of the 34th International Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems (NIPS '20).
